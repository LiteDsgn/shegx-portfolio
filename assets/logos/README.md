# Brand logos

Logos for the roles and brands sections. Reference them from `js/main.js`
(role `image` + `logo: true`) or the brands grid in `index.html`.

Naming: kebab-case, matching the brand. All current marks are WebP; transparent
PNG or SVG also work. White-on-dark marks sit on the dark logo tiles cleanly.

## Present
- `firecrackers-fc.webp`: Firecrackers FC crest (brands grid)
- `the-reborn-brand.webp`: The Reborn Brand (Reborn role card + brands grid)
- `talent-50.webp`: Talent 50 (Talent 50 role card + brands grid)
- `big-ben-socials.webp`: Big Ben's Socials Ltd. (brands grid)
- `kunta-nation.webp`: Kunta Nation (Kunta role card + brands grid)
